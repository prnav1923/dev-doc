# Acme Corp Employee Handbook
**Effective Date: January 1, 2026**
**Version: 4.2**

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to Acme Corp! We are delighted to have you on board. This handbook outlines the policies and procedures that guide our workplace. We believe in innovation, integrity, and impact.

## 2. Remote Work Policy
Acme Corp operates as a "Remote-First" company.
- **core Hours**: Employees are expected to be available for synchronous collaboration between 10:00 AM and 2:00 PM (EST), regardless of their physical location.
- **Stipend**: All full-time employees are eligible for a one-time Home Office Stipend of $1,500 USD upon joining.
- **Location**: You may work from anywhere within the country of your employment. International remote work requires prior approval from HR and is limited to 30 days per calendar year.

## 3. Expense & Travel Policy
We trust our employees to spend company money wisely.
- **General Rule**: Spend company money as if it were your own.
- **Meal Allowance**: $75 USD per day while traveling for business.
- **Flights**: Economy class for flights under 6 hours. Business class is permitted for international flights over 6 hours.
- **Approval**: Expenses over $500 require Manager approval. Expenses over $5,000 require VP approval.

## 4. Paid Time Off (PTO)
We believe in resting hard to work hard.
- **Vacation**: Acme Corp offers "Flexible PTO". There is no fixed cap, but we recommend a minimum of 3 weeks per year to prevent burnout.
- **Sick Leave**: 10 days of guaranteed paid sick leave per year.
- **Parental Leave**: 16 weeks of fully paid leave for all new parents (birth, adoption, or foster placement).

## 5. Security Protocols
- **Passwords**: All employees must use the company-provided Password Manager (AcmeSafe).
- **2FA**: Two-factor authentication is mandatory for all internal systems.
- **Devices**: Only company-issued laptops are allowed for accessing the production environment.

## 6. Code of Conduct
Acme Corp is committed to a harassment-free workplace. We have zero tolerance for discrimination based on race, gender, religion, or orientation.
- **Reporting**: Report violations anonymously via the "AcmeTrust" portal or directly to HR.

## 7. Performance Reviews
- **Cycle**: Performance reviews are conducted bi-annually in June and December.
- **Promotions**: Promotion cycles occur in January.
